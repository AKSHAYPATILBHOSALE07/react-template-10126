Separation and Purification in Pharmaceutical Industry

The Indian Pharmaceutical Industry is the third largest global API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient) merchant market. More than 200 nations across the world import pharmaceuticals from India. India aspires to become one of the top nations for drug innovation, according to the government’s PHARMA VISION 2020 plan.

Sanitech Engineers is well-positioned to provide the pharmaceutical sector cutting-edge technology and solutions to help it realise its growth ambition. We have a great deal of expertise working on fully turnkey projects and have a thorough grasp of the particular needs of the Indian pharmaceutical business. Our expertise is used to offer a wide selection of excellent technologies, including membrane systems and chromatography solutions.

API Purification

In order to produce high-quality medications and their intermediates, contaminants must be removed. The elimination of contaminants using traditional techniques of purification required laborious procedures including aqueous washes and liquid extraction. With our High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) equipment, Sanitech guarantees you very pure APIs and HPAPIs in less time and at a lower operational cost. We provide HPLC systems that are specially created to meet the client’s separation and process needs.
