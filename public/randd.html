R&D AND PROCESS DEVELOPMENT
INNOVATION

Process Development (PD) services involve downstream purification processes toward feasibility, optimization, and scale-up studies. At Sanitech Engineers, we can choose from a range of technologies and select an appropriate one that will efficiently produce your desired product quality. We also provide contractual manufacturing of the desired product. Our services play an important role in ensuring that the desired product is adapted to a strong platform that is scalable and creates a blueprint that delivers optimal yields and productivity. Investing time and money in our service ensures rapid development of your manufacturing process that will accelerate the process of getting your desired product to the market.

Our Process Development Service covers:
  • Selection of suitable membranes to meet the objective
  • Screening of resins used in low pressure liquid chromatography (LPLC)
  • Screening of stationary phase used in high pressure liquid chromatography (HPLC)
  • Batch, semi-continuous batch process development
  • Process optimization and reproducibility studies
  • Scale-up studies and data generation
