PREPARATIVE CHROMATOGRAPHY SYSTEMS

Static columns for Liquid Chromatography (Medium & Low Pressure)

The columns designed by Sanitech Engineers correspond to the highest requirements of preparative liquid chromatography. These columns are designed and packed using the sedimentation method.

BioPharmaceutical
Pharmaceutical
Peptide separation & purification

Sanitech columns are made of high-quality stainless steel (316 or 316L, depending on the request), and their inner surface is polished to reach Ra < 0,3 µm. Column flanges are not welded, but screwed onto the column tube. This means that there is no temperature increase during manufacture, and thus there is no change in steel properties. Sanitech columns are manufactured with legs consisting of stainless steel closed profiles that are fitted to the upper tube flange. This makes the output flange fully accessible for disassembly and sorbent release.


Sanitech columns are completed by a broad range of accessories, such as filling adapters, slurry mixing vessels, slurry transport pumps, etc., for automatic filling as well as automatic emptying procedures

Advantages of Static Columns for Liquid Chromatography

Versatile

Static columns are highly versatile and can be used for the separation of a wide range of analytes, including both small and large molecules.

Customizable

Static columns can be customized to meet specific analytical requirements, such as different classes of molecules or different stationary phases.

Consistency

Static columns offer high consistency, providing reproducible results with minimal variation between runs

High Efficiency

The stationary phase of static columns has a high surface area, which allows for the efficient separation of analytes in a sample.

Compatibility

Sanitech Engineers' static columns are compatible with a broad range of solvents and buffer systems, making them highly versatile.

Cost-effective

Static columns are highly cost-effective, providing high-quality separation at an affordable price.

High Resolution

Static columns provide a high-resolution separation of analytes in a sample, resulting in highly pure fractions.

Automation

Static columns are fully automatized

Sanitech Engineers offers a range of static columns for medium and low-pressure liquid chromatography, including silica-based and polymer-based columns. These columns provide excellent performance, reliability, and efficiency, making them ideal for a wide range of applications.


These applications include the separation, purification, and analysis of various types of biomolecules, such as proteins, nucleic acids, carbohydrates, and lipids. They are also used for the analysis of small molecules, such as pharmaceuticals.
