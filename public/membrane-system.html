MEMBRANE
SYSTEMS

Membrane systems are a subset of filtration technology that uses a semi-permeable membrane to separate materials according to their size, shape, or other properties. These systems offer an efficient method of separation, purification, or concentration in different industrial processes because they are built to selectively allow the passage of some molecules or particles while blocking others. Membrane systems are used in a variety of industries, including chemical manufacturing, pharmaceutical production, food and beverage processing, water treatment, and waste management.

Sanitech Engineers offers the most up-to-date recent membrane technologies that meet the different filtering demands of the process industries.
