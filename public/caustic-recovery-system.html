Caustic Recovery System

Caustic is used as a cleaning agent in several sectors, particularly food and dairy. The garbage created contributes to the ETP process, thus we must find a strategy to lessen this load. Caustic recovery is one option if the wasted caustic stream created after cleaning is treated separately. The caustic can be collected, lessening the burden on the ETP and allowing it to be reused for cleaning purposes. Sanitech Engineers has designed a membranebased green system with low monetory requirements, the ability to scale up and customise systems, and low maintenance costs.
