Welcome to Sanitech Engineers -

A Customized Membrane Filtration, and
Chromatography Systems Manufacturer

Sanitech Engineers specializes in the design and manufacture of high-purity water, membrane filtration/ separation, and chromatography purification systems for a variety of sectors, including pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, industrial biotech, nutraceutical, and food and beverages. Our engineering and project management teams continually strive to make new inventive and technology concepts a reality

Our R&D and process engineering teams assist our clients in moving novel separation and purification solutions from the lab to manufacturing. All of our advanced equipment are created in our cutting-edge production facilities and exceed the most stringent governmental guidelines. We have service teams situated throughout India that give exceptional after-sales support to our big pool of happy clients.
