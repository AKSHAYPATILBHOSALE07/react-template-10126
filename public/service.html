OUR SERVICES

Detailed Engineering and Automation Services


At Sanitech Engineers, we undertake complete turnkey projects including the design, selection, and development of the right technology, supply, installation, and commissioning, to meet the client's needs. We conduct comprehensive feasibility and/or pilot studies and then select the most suitable technology or combination of technologies for the project. We provide detailed engineering and automation services, including PLC control and control logic, and equipment layout to maximize shop floor space utilization. Further, we provide complete validation and documentation support required for operation under cGMP conditions and clean room environments. Our well-trained and experienced team conducts in-house FAT (Factory Acceptance Test) in the presence of customer representativevs to ensure smooth start-up and commissioning on site. Apart from this, through our extensive and efficient after-sales support and AMCs, we ensure that our customers are guaranteed the best and smooth performance.
