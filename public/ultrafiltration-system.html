Ultrafiltration System

Ultrafiltration is a filtration technology that uses porous films or materials with pores that help remove proteins and other biomolecules, polymers, and colloidal particles. Ultrafiltration membranes may retain species with molecular weights ranging from 10,000 to 500,000 daltons. It is a medium pressure process with typical operating pressures between 7 and 150 psi.

Sanitech Engineers’ Ultrafiltration systems are extremely easy to use and have a very long life expectancy.
