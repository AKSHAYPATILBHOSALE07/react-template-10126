Some of Its Salient Features

  • Approx. 300 IO’s handled per system

  • Batch reports for production, SIP & CIP, Calibration of load cells & passivation
  • Field Instruments & Equipments on Profinet & Profibus communication with S7-315 2PN-DP PLC for fast responses.

Case Study
Redundant SCADA

“Redundant SCADA as server- client architecture ” on a water system which had a total of around 12 distribution loops & around 5 generation system of Bosch German make(PWG+WFIG+PSG)

In an event of SCADA failure will result in complete shutdown of their water system of entire plant effecting production losses.

In case of failure of primary/Master SCADA server the redundant server comes online automatically to handle all client IPC’s to keep the entire system up & running. Absolutely zero data loss or down time of system even during down time of primary server. Customized reports & graphical group wise trends for all process parameters. Customized reports for generation system and distribution system of Bosch system. Customized Utility daily consumption report. 21 CFR Part 11 compliance for entire package including the remote 12 no:s IPC’s. Online data backup on FTP (File transfer protocol). Online hand shaking with DCS & NTP.
