Membrane Systems

Lab Scale System

With decades of separation and purifying experience handling a wide range of goods for a diverse client base, Sanitech Engineers has created a lab scale membrane-based solution. This lab size system is perfect for testing the viability of membrane-based processes and determining their applicability to fulfil the customer’s separation and purification goals. The data collected during the process development at the lab size may then be optimised and linearly scaled up to the production level. Our skilled experts carefully created this lab size system employing a scale down design technique. A system with a small footprint that can accommodate organic and inorganic membranes.
